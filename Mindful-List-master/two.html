Anxiety Burble

Natasha Tracy is a writer, speaker and social media consultant. She also has been suffering from dbipolar disorder since she has been 20. She often shares her stories in blogs to inspire and teach others. All of which she has written for have gained recognition and awards. Tracey is easy to relate to and shares many resources on mental health for her audience.



HERESE BORCHARD

Therese Borchard

Therese Borchard is a mental health writer and activist who primarily focuses on depression, nutrition and holistic health. She has founded an online community for people who struggle with depression, called, Project Hope and Beyond. She also regularly contributes to Everyday Health blogs.

The Anarchist Scoccer Mom

Liza Long writes and features mental health stories from the perspectives of parents. She shares a good balance of serious and fun blogs. She is also the author of The Price of Silence: A Moms Perspective on Mental Illness, a book about raising children with mental illness.
